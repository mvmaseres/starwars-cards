About us

Welcome to our StarWars card game! In this immersive experience, players can delve into the Star Wars universe to test their knowledge. The game offers a variety of guessing options, including characters, planets, movies, species, and starships. With three difficulty modes – Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced – each level provides a unique challenge, making the gameplay both entertaining and engaging.

Our primary data source for the game is the Star Wars API, affectionately known as "swapi." This API is the first quantified and programmatically accessible data source for all information in the canonical Star Wars universe. By leveraging this API, we can offer players a diverse range of options for guessing and exploring in our card game. For more details on how to interact with the API and the data available, visit swapi.dev.

The technologies employed in crafting this project encompass HTML, CSS, JavaScript These elements come together harmoniously to create a seamless and enjoyable user experience.

Throughout the development process, I encountered various challenges, including prioritization, structural design due to limited experience, and tackling design complexities. Each challenge presented an opportunity for growth and learning, allowing me to refine my skills and enhance the overall project.

When making design decisions, I drew inspiration from the Star Wars theme, incorporating its iconic color palette. Keeping the design simple ensures a clean and user-friendly experience for players.

This project is a personal endeavor, serving as a challenge and an addition to my portfolio. Despite the challenges faced, the development process was a rewarding journey.

Looking to the future, I aspire to enhance the overall design and introduce animations. Ideas include incorporating an animated plane that takes off to carry the suggestions form. Continuous user feedback is vital for implementing these improvements, ensuring the game evolves based on the needs and preferences of its players.
